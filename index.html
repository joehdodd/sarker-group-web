About Us

The Sarker Restaurant Group is opening Jersey Mike's Subs locations in the Pittsburgh Market. We are excited to help grow and develop the brand in the area. Like the original Mike's store in 1956, each Jersey Mike's Subs serves submarine sandwiches made to order, slicing the meats and cheeses as needed.

A popular way to top off the sandwich with condiments is to order it "Mike's Way", which involves sliced onions, shredded lettuce, tomatoes, oregano, salt (spices) and "The Juice" - a mixture of red wine vinegar and olive oil. We hope you join us in this wave of growth as Jersey Mike's has 1,800 stores and growing!
